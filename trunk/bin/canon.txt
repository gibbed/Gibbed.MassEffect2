importing one, everything else is false:

[Virmire] Rescued Kaidan, if Shepard is female
[Citadel] Abandoned the Council
[Citadel] Chose Udina
[Henchmen] Garrus recruited
[Henchmen] Wrex recruited
[Virmire] Rana Thanoptis is dead
